Education/Certifications

Education

ABRA- Managers License

The ABRA Beverage License, is a license issued to to ABC managers in the District. An ABC manager is required to be on duty and on the premises during the approved licensed hours of sales in order for the establishment to sell and serve alcoholic beverages.

Food and Safety Handlers Certified

The Food and Safety Handlers Certification, is the card/permit that is used as official documentation to show to health inspectors that a food and safety course has been completed.

TIPS Certified

A TIPS Certification is a skills-based, alcohol training and certification program that is designed to prevent intoxication, underage drinking, and drunk driving by enhancing the fundamental "people skills" of servers, sellers and consumers of alcohol.
